Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Kesaribyshammirandhawa.com is the official website for Kesari by Shammi Randhawa, a bespoke Indian couture boutique in Chandigarh, India. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the website or contact us through the channels shown on the site.

Information We Collect

We do not sell products directly through this website and we do not collect payment information here. We may receive information in these ways:

  • Information you choose to share when contacting us by Instagram, Facebook, phone, or any future contact form.
  • Basic website usage information collected through Google Analytics, such as page views, approximate location, device type, browser, referral source, and interaction data.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser settings, and cookie identifiers used for analytics and website security.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, understand website traffic, improve the website experience, and help visitors discover relevant boutique and collection information.

Google Analytics And Cookies

This website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use the website. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect usage information such as page views, device type, approximate location, referral source, and interaction data. Google may process this information according to its own policies. You can learn more at Google's partner sites policy.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You may also use Google's browser add-on to opt out of Google Analytics where available.

Third-Party Links

The website links to third-party platforms, including Instagram and Facebook. When you open those links, your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies, terms, and cookie practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate the website, analytics, hosting, or communication channels. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect legal rights and safety.

Data Retention

Inquiry information is kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, manage appointments, maintain boutique records, or comply with legal and business requirements. Analytics data retention is managed within Google Analytics settings.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have directly shared with us, subject to applicable legal and business requirements. You can also limit cookies through your browser.
